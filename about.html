Our Story

A Legacy of Learning in Aurangabad

Holy Angels Public School (HAPS) was founded with a singular belief — that every child in Aurangabad deserves access to quality, values-based education. What began as a small school in Maharana Pratap Nagar has grown, over nearly three decades, into one of the most trusted CBSE schools in the region.

Our journey has been shaped by generations of students, dedicated teachers, and parents who chose to trust us with the most important years of their children's lives. That trust is something we honour every single day.

With our second campus at Nawgarh Moad, Poiwan, we now serve a wider community — bringing the same standard of education, care and discipline closer to more families in Aurangabad.

Purpose & Direction

Vision & Mission

🌟

Our Vision

To nurture responsible, confident and compassionate individuals equipped with knowledge, skills and values to succeed in a changing world — and to contribute meaningfully to their families, communities and nation.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide holistic education through a balanced focus on academics, discipline, values, sports and co-curricular activities — in a safe, supportive environment where every child can discover their potential.

A Message from Leadership

Principal's Message

👩‍🏫
Education is a partnership — between the school, parents, and every child who walks through our doors.

At HAPS, we believe that a child's school years are formative not just academically, but in character and values. Our dedicated teachers strive to make learning meaningful, engaging and joyful — while instilling the habits of discipline, honesty and self-belief.

We do not merely prepare students for examinations. We prepare them for life. Every activity, every lesson, every interaction in our school is designed with this larger purpose in mind.

We warmly invite you to visit either of our campuses, meet our team, and experience the HAPS environment for yourself.

The Principal Holy Angels Public School, Aurangabad
