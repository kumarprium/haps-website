Admission Process

5 Simple Steps to Join HAPS

  1. 1

    Call or Visit the School

    Call either campus or walk in during school hours to ask about seat availability. Our staff will guide you through the process.

    📞 Main: 9931921825 · Poiwan: 9128614032
  2. 2

    Campus Visit

    Come to the school with your child and family. Take a campus tour, meet our teaching staff and understand our approach to education at HAPS.

  3. 3

    Collect & Submit Admission Form

    Collect the admission form from the school office. Fill it completely and submit it along with the required documents listed on the right.

  4. 4

    Assessment / Interaction

    For all classes from LKG onwards, students may be asked for a brief written or oral interaction with our academic team. This helps us understand the child's current level and ensure they receive the right support. The final admission decision rests with the school.

    ℹ️ Nursery is exempt from assessment
  5. 5

    Confirmation & Fee Payment

    On confirmation of admission, complete fee payment at the school office. Your child's seat is reserved only on completion of this step. Welcome to HAPS!
