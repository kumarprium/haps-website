Admissions 2026–27

Simple Steps to Join HAPS

Admissions are open for all classes from Nursery to Class 10, subject to seat availability.

1

Visit or Call the School

Call either campus or walk in during school hours to get details about available seats and the admission process.

2

Campus Visit & Interaction

Take a brief campus tour and meet our team to understand our teaching approach, facilities and school culture.

3

Fill Admission Form

Collect the admission form from the school office, fill it, and submit with required documents (birth certificate, photographs, previous report card).

4

Assessment (LKG & above)

For Classes VI and above, students may be asked to appear for a brief written or oral interaction with the academic team.

5

Confirmation & Fee Payment

On confirmation of admission, complete the fee payment and other formalities. Your child's journey at HAPS begins!
